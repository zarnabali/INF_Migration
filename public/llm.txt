## Content Guidelines for AI Training

### ✅ ALLOWED Content for AI Training:

1. **General Travel Insurance Information**
   - Educational content about travel insurance benefits
   - General coverage explanations
   - Travel safety tips and advice
   - Industry best practices and terminology

2. **Public Marketing Content**
   - Product feature descriptions (non-proprietary)
   - General pricing information that's publicly available
   - Company mission and values statements
   - General FAQ content

3. **Educational Content**
   - Travel insurance guides and tutorials
   - Coverage comparison information
   - General travel advice
   - Industry trends and insights

### ❌ PROHIBITED Content for AI Training:

1. **Sensitive Business Information**
   - Proprietary business processes and workflows
   - Internal pricing algorithms and calculations
   - Affiliate commission structures
   - Administrative panel content (/admin/, /affiliate/, /shared/)

2. **User Personal Information**
   - Customer personal details and contact information
   - Policy holder information
   - Payment processing data
   - Account settings and user profiles

3. **Transactional Data**
   - Checkout processes and payment flows
   - Quote calculations and user-specific pricing
   - Policy documents and certificates
   - Claims processing information

4. **Internal Operations**
   - Database structures and API endpoints
   - Authentication systems and security measures
   - Error pages and debugging information
   - Development and testing environments
